About Us

Welcome to DogsHaven - the premier dog-care centre for busy pet owners!

Whether you need day-care services for your furry friend while you're at work, or just want to treat your dog to a fun-filled day of exercise and socialization, DogsHaven has got you covered.

Our state-of-the-art facility features a range of amenities designed to keep your dog happy, healthy, and entertained, including outdoor space, a 'gym' area, and even a pool!

Our team of experienced and qualified dog-carers and trainers will ensure that your dog receives the best possible care and attention, while you can relax and focus on your own work or errands.
